About TrueFix Wireless

TrueFix Wireless specializes in professional electronic repairs with honest pricing and quality workmanship. Customers can choose budget, OEM, or premium replacement parts depending on their needs. We strive to provide fast turnaround times, excellent communication, and reliable repair services.

TrueFix Wireless is not responsible for damage that occurs during shipping to our repair location. Return shipping estimates may vary depending on carrier performance and location.

Repair Policy

By submitting a device for repair, customers authorize diagnostic and repair work. Some devices may reveal additional issues once opened. Water damaged devices cannot be guaranteed to be fully recoverable. Estimated repair times are subject to parts availability and device condition.

Warranty Policy

Warranty coverage applies only to replacement parts installed by TrueFix Wireless. Physical damage, liquid damage, accidental damage, misuse, neglect, software issues, or repairs performed by others after service may void warranty coverage. Warranty does not cover unrelated failures that occur after repair.
