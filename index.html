About TrueFix Wireless

TrueFix Wireless is dedicated to providing professional electronic repair services with honest pricing, clear communication, and quality workmanship. Customers can choose budget, OEM, or premium replacement parts depending on their needs and budget.

📦 Shipping Information

Customers are responsible for securely packaging devices before shipping. Tracking numbers and shipping insurance are highly recommended. TrueFix Wireless is not responsible for shipping damage that occurs during transit to our repair location.

🔧 Repair Policy

By submitting a device for repair, customers authorize diagnostic and repair work. Additional issues may be discovered after opening a device. Water damaged devices cannot be guaranteed to be fully recoverable.

🛡️ Warranty Policy

Warranty applies only to replacement parts installed by TrueFix Wireless. Physical damage, liquid damage, misuse, neglect, or repairs performed by others after service may void warranty coverage.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Customer information is collected only for repair services, communication, invoicing, and customer support. TrueFix Wireless does not sell customer information to third parties.
